    The Art of Zootopia 2

    The Art of Zootopia 2 offers a comprehensive exploration of the visual development and production process behind Walt Disney Animation Studios’ follow-up to the 2016 original. As a central installment in the long-running Art of Disney series, this volume documents the creative evolution required to expand the diverse ecosystems and characters of the Zootopia universe.
